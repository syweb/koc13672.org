Welcome to Knights of Columbus

The Knights of Columbus Neil W. Seidl Council is a fraternal order of men dedicated in our Catholic faith and operating continuously since October 2004.

Four Principals guide our service to community:
Charity + Unity + Fraternity + Patriotism

We strive to overcome the hardships through service to our youth, family, Church and community. Social and intellectual fellowship is promoted among members and their families through educational, charitable, religious, social welfare, and public relief works.
